Welcome to the LexClassify app. To help you get started, I've created a short guide below.

1. Adding your classes:

  • Click on the tab that says Courses.
  • To create a new class, simply click Add a new course".
  • Enter your class name, room number, and click to select the blocks during which your class meets.
  • When you're done, just click "Create" to add the class.
  • This will take you back to the Course tab, where you'll see a list of your classes.
  • You can always edit your classes simply by clicking on the class names.

2. Adding lunches:

  • Click on the tab that says Courses.
  • Click the green Edit button to begin adding your lunches.
  • To save your selections, just click Submit to save, or Cancel to reset your choices.

3. Viewing:

  • Click on the tab that says Courses.
  • I have provided a monthly, weekly and daily view for you to see your classes, and you can navigate the days by clicking "previous" or "next".
